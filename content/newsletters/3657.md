<p>📷 Road (#mbmar Micro Blog March photo challenge prompt suggested by <a href="https://micro.blog/Dejus">@Dejus</a>)</p>

<p>So we keep the ungraded and bumpy dirt roads as an instrument of faith in the neighbours we want.</p> 
<p><img src="https://joshwithers.blog/uploads/2023/32c88135ed.jpg" width="450" height="600" alt="" style="max-width: 100%; height: auto;"><br> <a href="https://joshwithers.blog/2023/03/16/road-mbmar-micro.html" class="microblog_permalink">2023-03-16</a></p> <hr> 
<p>A <a href="https://www.weforum.org/agenda/2023/02/critical-thinking-ignoring-brain/">bunch of smart people</a> say we need to learn "‘critical ignoring’ – the ability to choose what to ignore and where to invest our limited attentional capacities."<br> <a href="https://joshwithers.blog/2023/03/16/a-bunch-of.html" class="microblog_permalink">2023-03-16</a></p> <hr> 
<p>Everyone move along, there’s nothing to see here.</p> 
<p>The “crack” on Josh’s iPhone screen that has occupied his sad mind for the last two hours was actually the smallest dried strand of egg yolk draped across his screen, hard enough and thin enough to seem like a crack to the untrained mind.</p> 
<p>If only you could’ve seen the look on my face as I scratched off the scratch like a miracle healer.<br> <a href="https://joshwithers.blog/2023/03/16/everyone-move-along.html" class="microblog_permalink">2023-03-17</a></p> <hr>
