I’ve always wanted to see my work published, so when my friend Justin suggested I had been in a photo book called “Australia: A Celebration”, I went investigating.

![](https://joshwithers.blog/uploads/2022/52679ed977.jpg)

$16! What a bargain!

![](https://joshwithers.blog/uploads/2022/2e18efa611.jpg)

Oh, there I am.

![](https://joshwithers.blog/uploads/2022/3ee7633bbb.jpg)
![](https://joshwithers.blog/uploads/2022/e97a07ad30.jpg)

If you’re liking these photos, I reckon you’d really like my [Unsplash portfolio](https://unsplash.com/@joshwithers) where you can use all the images for free.

You just can’t sell them.

![](https://joshwithers.blog/uploads/2022/e43b8ceae2.jpg)
![](https://joshwithers.blog/uploads/2022/f686f4e85c.jpg)

And there I’m not. No attribution, at the least.

![](https://joshwithers.blog/uploads/2022/f4ccf45a0e.jpg)

No permission sought, or license payment made.

Off to the lawyers we go.
