It’s by [@martinfeld](https://micro.blog/martinfeld) of [@HemisphericViews](https://micro.blog/HemisphericViews) on “the narratives of tech-podcast fandom and the role of open RSS”. Two of my favourite episodes so far are with [@marco](https://micro.blog/marco) and [@jsnell](https://micro.blog/jsnell).
