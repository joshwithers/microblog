My favourite thing to do in the big cities of the world is to ignore the must-do lists, the must-see places, the hotspots and the icons, and to just walk around and exist in a different big city. Walking down random streets, getting bad coffee at little-known cafes, and finding the unseen parts of a city.
The towers, cathedrals, arches and museums are cool and we inevitably end up there.
But there’s something really interesting to me about experiencing another people’s normal.
