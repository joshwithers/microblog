For an interesting NASA and Apple-related fall down a rabbit hole, start with the [origin of the name](https://sb.longnow.org/SB_homepage/WholeEarth_buton.html) of "The Whole Earth Catalog" in 1966, skip forward to [1972 when a whole earth photo was made](https://qz.com/458826/behold-nasas-first-image-in-decades-of-the-whole-earth).

<img src="uploads/2023/ad0cb9dc712fc3cc65394407787880d2.jpg.webp" width="600" height="598" alt="Photo of the whole earth made in 1972">

Then take a turn to one of Steve Jobs' favourite sayings ["Stay hungry stay foolish"](https://www.flickr.com/photos/tracilawson/6216133338) which [he quoted in 2005 at Stanford](https://news.stanford.edu/2005/06/12/youve-got-find-love-jobs-says/) in his famous commencement speech.

<img src="uploads/2023/6216133338-f9d42c9839-o.jpg" width="440" height="600" alt="Back page of the last Whole Earth Catalog magazine: Stay hungry, stay foolish">

Ad then wrap back around to how the [whole earth image as an iPhone wallpaper came to be](https://earthobservatory.nasa.gov/blogs/elegantfigures/2011/10/06/crafting-the-blue-marble/).

<img src="uploads/2023/iphone-2007.jpg" width="322" height="600" alt="Original iPhone with earth background">
