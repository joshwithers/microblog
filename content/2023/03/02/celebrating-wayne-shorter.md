Celebrating Wayne Shorter ([1933-2023](https://tedgioia.substack.com/p/remembering-wayne-shorter-1933-2023)) with his [2018 open letter](https://www.herbiehancock.com/2016/12/10/an-open-letter-to-the-next-generation-of-artists/) co-authored with Herbie Hancock:

> "We are all pieces in a giant, fluid puzzle, where the smallest of actions by one puzzle piece profoundly affects each of the others. You matter, your actions matter, your art matters."
