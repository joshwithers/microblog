Pentax has made a portrait-orientation, made for sharing on modern social media, analog film camera in the new [Pentax 17](https://pentax.eu/products/pentax-17).

[DPReview](https://www.dpreview.com/news/9002061674/ricoh-launches-pentax-17-half-frame-fixed-lens-film-camera):

> The Pentax 17 has a 25mm F3.5 lens which works out at 37mm equivalent, and derives its name from the horizontal width of the 17 x 25mm frames it captures. The company says the vertical format makes it similar to images shot by smartphones.

What a time to be alive.
